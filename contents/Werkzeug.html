HASHING PASSWORDS:

Two main functions of this job are 'generate_password_hash' and 'check_password_hash':


import werkzeug.security

a = werkzeug.security.generate_password_hash(password='12345', method='pbkdf2:sha256', salt_length=16)
b = werkzeug.security.check_password_hash(pwhash=a, password='12345')
c = werkzeug.security.check_password_hash(pwhash=a, password='123')

print(b)
print(c)
