Calculate the T-test:

T-test is a statistical test used to compare the means of two groups. The result of a t-test is a p-value. A small p-value (typically less than 0.05) indicates that it is unlikely that the observed difference between the means occurred by chance . In other words, the difference in means is highly statistically significant and we can go ahead on publish our research paper.


import scipy.stats as stats

stats.ttest_ind(a=series_of_data_1, b=series_of_data_2)
